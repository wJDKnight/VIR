How it works - Simple Steps
- Set up a device and set buffer time (5 seconds delay)
- Perform your action (shoot)
- 5 seconds later watch your performance played back on the screen
- (Optional) double tap screen or press the volume down button to mark a key point in the video for later clipping
- Immediately adjust your form based on what you saw
- Repeat the cycle for continuous improvement
- after stop, the video are clipped into segments based on the key points
- popup a target face, user can mark where the arrow hit the target, then get the overall score of the round
- the clipped videos can be linked with which hit in the target
- Replay in slow-motion for frame-by-frame analysis
